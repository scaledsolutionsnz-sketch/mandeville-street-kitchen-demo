About us

A neighbourhood kitchen, not a chain

Mandeville Street Kitchen is a small, independent café built around a simple idea: real food, made with care, in a room that feels like your own. We keep the menu tight so everything's done well, and we cook the way we'd cook at home — just with better coffee.

Whether you're in for a quiet flat white, a long weekend brunch or feeding a hungry crew, you'll get a genuine Christchurch welcome and a plate worth coming back for.

  • Locally owned and run on Mandeville Street
  • Seasonal menu using local produce
  • Dietary-friendly options, just ask the team
