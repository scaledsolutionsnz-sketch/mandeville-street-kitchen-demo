Our kitchen

About us

A neighbourhood kitchen, not a chain

Mandeville Street Kitchen is a small, locally run café in Christchurch built around honest food and a good cup of coffee. The kind of place where the regulars have a usual and there's always a friendly face behind the counter.

We keep the menu short so we can do it well — everything cooked to order, baking out of our own oven, and nothing pretending to be fancier than it is. Whether you're after a quiet breakfast, a catch-up over coffee or a quick lunch, you're welcome here.
