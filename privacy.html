Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mandeville Street Kitchen respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it. This is placeholder content for the demonstration site and should be reviewed by the business before going live.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a booking, send an enquiry or leave feedback through this website, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, email address, phone number and any message you send us. We only collect what we need to respond to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your booking requests and enquiries.
  • To manage reservations, functions and catering orders.
  • To improve our food, service and overall experience based on your feedback.

3. How We Store It

Form submissions on this site are delivered to us by email through a third-party form service. We keep your information only as long as needed to assist you and do not sell or trade your personal details to anyone.

4. Cookies & Analytics

This website may use basic cookies and analytics to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without losing access to the site's core information.

5. Your Choices

You can ask us what information we hold about you, request a correction, or ask us to delete it. Just get in touch using the details below and we'll help.

6. Contact
