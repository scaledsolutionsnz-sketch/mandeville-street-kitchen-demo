Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mandeville Street Kitchen respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect through this website and how we handle it, in line with the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand).

What we collect

When you send an enquiry, booking request or feedback through this site, we collect only what you give us — typically your name, your contact details (phone or email) and your message. We don't ask for anything we don't need.

How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry, confirm a booking or respond to your feedback
  • To keep a record of your request so we can follow up if needed

We do not sell or rent your information, and we won't use it for marketing without your consent.

How it's handled

Enquiry forms on this site are delivered to us by a third-party form service. Your message is sent to our email so we can respond. We keep your details only as long as needed to deal with your enquiry.

Cookies and analytics

This is a simple informational website. It does not set advertising cookies. Any basic, anonymous usage statistics are used only to understand how the site is performing.

Your rights

You can ask us what personal information we hold about you and request that we correct it. Contact us and we'll help.
